Partners

Shield the Bay has partnered with Oakstop Alliance, a nonprofit organization created to further serve the communities of the East Bay with free to low cost space and experiences.

Oakstop Alliance is committed to providing affordable workspaces to serve as COVID-19 response centers. For these past few months, Oakstop Alliance has supported Shield the Bay response efforts by hosting and serving as our PPE production and assembly site for all our face shields for healthcare workers. Not only have they generously shared their space with us, but they also share our values, which is critical to us in this partnership: The Oakstop Alliance supports groups “that confront and deconstruct the barriers of systematic oppression with actionable results”. Working closely together, we have assembled hundreds of face shields. Thanks to Oakstop Alliance Executive Director Damon Johnson for welcoming us in and embracing our movement.

To connect and learn more about Oakstop Alliance click here.

For further information contact Executive Director Damon Johnson: damon@oakstop.com
